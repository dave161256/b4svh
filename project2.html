Gigabit Vouchers

The aim of our project is to provide fast, reliable broadband for everybody that wants it in our community by building our own full-fibre gigabit network with the help of B4RN.

As a member of our community, you can play your part by applying for a Gigabit Voucher. Your voucher application will raise up to £1500 (or up to £3500 if you apply as a business) towards the cost of building our network. The government wants to give us the funds to help us to build our network, all you have to do is apply
