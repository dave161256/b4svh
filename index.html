Find out about VoIP

With a full fibre connection you will be able to make phonecalls over the internet using VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) so you will not need a phone-line any more.

B4RN and the primary school

One of the benefits to our community of working with B4RN to build our fibre network is that the primary school will get the gigabit service from B4RN free.
